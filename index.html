ASCO Posters & Abstracts

ASCO 2025 · Poster / Abstract

Sex and gender influence on adverse events for melanoma patients

Meta-analysis of 69 studies shows a higher risk of thyroid-related adverse events in women undergoing melanoma treatments, supporting the need for sex-specific monitoring.

Insights from a survey in an Italian cancer referral center. Survey results show high public acceptance of AI in health care, with concerns mainly related to reliability, privacy, and reduced human interaction.

International Conference · Biostatistics

M-SKIP Project: Melanoma risk and mole count across age groups

A pooled analysis showed that a high number of common nevi increases melanoma risk similarly across age groups, while atypical nevi are a much stronger risk factor in younger individuals. These findings highlight the importance of age-specific risk stratification for targeted prevention. Presented at the 32nd International Biometric Conference, Atlanta
